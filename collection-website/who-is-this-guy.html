Who is this guy?


For those who only know a few things about Yu-Gi-Oh, or for those who don't know at all, you may be wondering,

Who is Utopia/Hope?


Utopia is one of the first xyz monsters to debut. He originated in the 4th installment of the Yu-Gi-Oh franchise, "Yu-Gi-Oh! Zexal". He's the ace monster of the protagonist of the series, Yuma Tsukumo, this makes him the main monster of the series.

Utopia is an angel warrior, white body covered in golden armor matched with strong angelic looking wings,paired with a matching sword Utopia is ready to fight and defend.

More Info


Utopia is used by our protagonist of the show Yuma Tsukumo and his spirit partner Astral
Utopia is used to both show Yuma's journey of becoming a better duelist and to represent the bond of Yuma and Astral and how it grows stronger to the end.
