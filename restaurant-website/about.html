About Us



La Chelita is a Mexican restaurant located in Austin Texas, this family owned restaurant has lasted generations connected to its community. Very locals friendly and VERY family friendly. La Chelita serves traditional Mexican food based off Jalisco, they specialize in fresh handmade tortillas.





About the Owner



Jessica, the current owner, is the granddaughter to the creator of the restaurant Graciela "Chelita" Ramírez. It’s Jessica’s mission to honor her grandmother while also showing off her culinary vision inspired by her Mexican-American culture. A challenge that Jessica is facing is trying to figure out how she can attract new faces to her restaurant while also keeping her loyal customers that been apart of La Chelita’s history for years.
